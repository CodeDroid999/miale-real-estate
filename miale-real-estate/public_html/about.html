About

All about Maile Real Estate

Who We Are?

At Miale Real Estate, we are a dedicated team of real estate professionals with a long-standing reputation a the go-to real-estate company in Kenya.We empower you dreams.

What We Do?

We specialize in a singular focus: Land. We are experts in providing value-added plots in prime locations, complete with essential amenities such as accessible roads and electricity.

Why We Do It?

We do what we do because we believe in the transformative power of land. Land is the foundation upon which dreams are built, legacies are forged, and communities are nurtured.

Since When?

2020.Since our inception two years ago, we have been dedicated to excellence and innovation in the world of real estate. We have helped 200+ clients invest in land in prime locations.
