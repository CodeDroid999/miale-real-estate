Oasis Gardens

Location: Thigio, Kikuyu

Size: 50 * 100

Status: Available

Features:

  • Electricity
  • Water
  • Accessible Roads
  • Proximity to Social Amenities

Oasis Gardens offers prime plots suitable for residential developments in Thigio, Kikuyu. The project is conveniently situated just 1.3 kilometers from the tarmac road (Dagoretti-Mutarakwa Road) and is approximately 37 kilometers from Nairobi CBD, making it an accessible and well-connected location.

This project provides essential amenities to ensure residents’ comfortable and convenient living experiences. Electricity is readily available on-site, ensuring a reliable power supply for your residential needs. The project also offers a dependable water supply on-site, ensuring residents have access to clean and sufficient water, as well as access to social amenities.

Payment Terms

Cash Offer: Ksh 750,000

Installment: Ksh. 850,000 (Deposit: 400,000, Balance in 3 months)
